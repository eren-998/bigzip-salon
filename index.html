Sabse Bada Secret: Newton Ka 3rd Law

Action ka Reaction!

Rocket science ka sabse bada raaz Newton ke 3rd law me chupa hai: "Har action ka ek equal aur opposite reaction hota hai."

Socho, jab aap ek balloon se hawa chhodte ho, to hawa niche jaati hai (action) aur balloon upar bhaagta hai (reaction). Rocket bilkul aise hi kaam karta hai! Bas usme hawa ki jagah bahut tezi se jalti hui gas (exhaust) nikalti hai.

Kuch Aur Zaroori Baatein

Staging Kya Hai?

Lamba safar tay karne ke liye, rocket apne extra wajan (jaise khaali ho chuke fuel tanks) ko raaste me gira deta hai. Isse rocket halka ho jaata hai aur aage tezi se badhta hai. Isi ko staging kehte hain.

Escape Velocity

Dharti ki gravity (gurutvakarshan) se hamesha ke liye azaad hone ke liye rocket ko ek minimum speed pakadni padti hai. Ye speed hai lagbhag 11.2 km/s (ya 40,000 km/h)!

Orbit Me Kaise Rehte Hain?

Satellite orbit me isliye rehta hai kyunki wo aage ki taraf bahut tezi se jaa raha hai, jabki dharti use niche kheench rahi hai. In dono ka balance use hamesha dharti ke chakkar kaatne par majboor kar deta hai.
